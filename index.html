Printer Guide 2025: Types, Specifications, and Must-Have Features

Choosing the printer in 2025 becomes easier when you are fully aware of the various types, specifications, and features that are there. This simple guide will provide you with all the information so that your choice can be the best that suits your needs. There are many types of printers. Inkjet printers are great for regular use, especially for colorful images. Laser printers are perfect for quick, large-volume printing and are also mostly used in offices. Scanner and copier all-in-one printers are best for multitasking. Specifications-wise, you will want to keep an eye on print speed (number of pages per minute), resolution (DPI for sharpness), and monthly duty cycle (whatever amount of print can handle in one month). In case you are printing a lot of papers, then getting a high-speed laser printer with a huge paper tray is a better option.
